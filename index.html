Slabstack Admin — clickable prototype
Behavior demo, not final visual QA
A simulated "Enable Tiered Pricing" organization-wide setting locks the rule's Tiered Price toggle with an inline hint when off, and turns its below/above configuration read-only (values preserved, but disabled) if a rule already has Tiered Price selected. An "or" indicator sits between the two mutually-exclusive toggles ("Require approval for all quotes" and "Require approval based on Tier Pricing deviation") — not shown near Bypass, since it isn't part of that exclusion. The standard criterion configurator lives directly under "Require approval for all quotes" as removable cards ("+ Add threshold criteria" appends another), and only appears while that toggle is off and Tiered Price is not selected — it's fully hidden, not just dimmed, once either toggle is on. Each tier direction (below/above) has its own $/% unit toggle and a required Deviation threshold, and at least one direction must stay on. Turning "Enable Tiered Pricing" off while rules still reference Tiered Price shows a warning linking to the affected rule(s), which scrolls to and highlights the drawer when clicked. Steps 1–3, the rules table, and the deactivate-rule flow remain out of scope.
